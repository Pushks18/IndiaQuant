  • Use MIS (intraday) product, not CNC. 5x leverage, auto-square-off at 15:20. Margins shown above assume MIS.
  • Don't trade the first 15 min. Half of all retail intraday losses happen in the open auction noise.
  • Exit by 15:15. Auto-square-off slippage at 15:20 is real money lost.
  • If 9:30 range > 1.5 × ATR estimate, the day is unusually volatile — skip or halve qty.
  • Don't average down. If a position is at stop, exit. Adding to a loser is the most expensive habit in trading.
